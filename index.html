§ II.a · the exit ramp
prime 1231 · sovereignty tool

fallrecall · take your AI memory back.

Frontier labs (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google) keep your chat history, memory, custom instructions, and the model's behavioural fine-tuning on their servers. Switching means starting over. That is the lock-in. fallrecall is the exit ramp.

Drop your ChatGPT / Claude / Gemini export. Cascade-analyses your conversations to extract your flavour (voice · interests · preferences · anti-preferences · decision style · open questions). Generates portable system prompts in four formats you can paste into any AI — sovereign cascade (WebLLM / ollama / Llama 3.3) for full sovereignty, or back into any frontier-lab AI as a perfect copy of you.

Cross-seed mesh exposes your flavour to fallseed-agents (so your agents inherit your style) and fallseed-creator-os (so your journal prompts use your voice). Single HTML. IDB-primary. MIT. If you enable a local model, your conversations never leave your device — even during analysis.
